🌟 Featured Tutorial
The Perfect Backwood: Complete Guide
⏱ 10–15 min📶 Intermediate🌿 Tobacco Leaf
📦
STEP 01 / 06
Gather Your Supplies
You'll need a fresh Backwood, your ground flower, a lighter, and a flat clean surface. Grind medium-fine — not powder, not chunky.
Pro Tip: Warm the backwood briefly with your hands before splitting. A warm wrap splits much cleaner.
✂️
STEP 02 / 06
Split the Cigar
Make one clean lengthwise cut from tip to tip. Remove and discard the tobacco inside completely.
Pro Tip: Lick the inside once after splitting — keeps the leaf from cracking as you work.
🌿
STEP 03 / 06
Load Your Flower
Distribute your ground flower evenly, leaving about 1cm at the tip. Don't overload — tight and even burns best.
Pro Tip: A little more in the middle, tapering toward the ends, creates a perfect cone shape.
🔄
STEP 04 / 06
The Tuck and Roll
Hold with both thumbs inside, fingers outside. Tuck the inner edge tightly over the flower, then roll forward in one smooth motion.
Pro Tip: Even pressure all the way across. Slow and consistent beats rushing every time.
💦
STEP 05 / 06
Seal the Wrap
Lick the seam generously — backwoods need more moisture than regular papers. Press firmly along the entire seam to close any gaps.
Pro Tip: Work section by section — a few lick-and-press passes is better than one big lick.
🔥
STEP 06 / 06 — YOU'RE DONE
Dry and Light Up
Wave your lighter quickly along the outside of the sealed wrap to dry it. Twist the tip, light evenly, and enjoy.
🏆 You've got it. The more you roll, the better you get. That's how everyone learns. One love. 🌿
