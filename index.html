Who We Are

THE HEALING
HUB

MadaKop is a private members-only cannabis club and healing hub based in the Vaal, serving Small Farm, Evaton, Sebokeng and surrounding areas. We operate privately — no public sales, no public promotion. Just real people, real community, and responsible cannabis culture.

Founded in 2025, MadaKop is built on the values of love, respect, safety, and community. We welcome all adults who embrace those same values.

CLUB RULES

Open 8AM – 8PM · All members must follow these rules to keep MadaKop safe, legal, and welcoming for everyone.

01
Members must be 18 years or older. No exceptions.
02
The club operates privately — no public sales or promotion of cannabis.
03
All cannabis is for personal and responsible use only.
04
Sharing outside the club or resale is strictly prohibited.
05
Respect the privacy and confidentiality of all members at all times.
06
No minors allowed on the premises or at any club event. Period.
07
Maintain cleanliness and respect for club spaces and fellow members.
08
Consumption is allowed only in designated areas within the club.
09
Members must follow all South African cannabis laws and club policies.
10
The club reserves the right to suspend or terminate membership for violations.
